What are lash extensions?

Lash extensions are not the same as the strip lashes you can buy in the store. They are semi-permanent, synthetic lashes that are glued to your natural lashes through the use of lash adhesive (a special lash glue). They are placed on the lash individually, where you can complete different looks.

How do I prepare for lash extensions?

How often should I get my lashes refilled?

As humans, we have between 90-120 natural lashes on our upper lash line. However, these will shed daily, an average of 2-5 lashes. We recommend booking an appointment for infills every 2-4 weeks depending on the person.
